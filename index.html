SPECIALIZATIONS

DEEP CLEANING

  • Dust and vacuum: We clean Hard-to-reach ledges, windows, light fixtures and above cabinets. Basically, all the places that are too hard to reach during your normal cleaning routine.
  • Faucets: We De-scale faucets / shower-heads throughout the home with vinegar.
  • Vent Covers: We remove HVAC vent covers and wash them.
  • Windows: We vacuum windowsills and window tracks and remove cobwebs and bugs from window screens
  • Doors: We wipe down doors and door frames for fingerprints and smudges.
  • Garbage Cans: We wipe out and sanitize garbage cans, recycling bins and wastebaskets.
  • Blinds: We give your blinds a deep clean by spraying them with vinegar and scrubbing them down.
  • Couch & Chairs: We remove all cushions and vacuum the creases where pet hair and popcorn love to call home.

SPECIALISED KITCHEN APPLIANCES, OVEN AND STOVETOP CLEANING

  • Oven: Before you crank up the heat on your “self-cleaning” oven, let’s get out all the stuff that can be a fire hazard.
  • Stovetop: We remove the pot grates from the stovetop and soak in hot soapy water.
  • Microwave: We grease that elbow, loosen the food splatters
  • Toaster: We remove the fire hazard (also known as breadcrumbs)
  • Refrigerator/Freezer: We clean rubber gasket around the door seals with warm soapy water and then disinfect.
